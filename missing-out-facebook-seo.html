Business owners are missing out on one of the biggest search engines!

Facebook is the biggest social media platform in the world. However, what most people don't understand is that Facebook is also a search engine. People don't just search for other people on Facebook. They also search for products, places, and groups.

How is Facebook Search Structured?

On top of the results, you almost always find Facebook groups and sometimes Facebook pages. However, if you scroll down a bit, what do we see?

The latest Facebook posts, or Facebook posts that people interact with.

The more "SEO" optimized your content is, the more searches you can get. The more frequently you post, the more likely someone searching for something in particular will be able to find your products.

Facebook is continuously growing and people keep using it. By leveraging all of the features on Facebook, from events to posts, you will get more exposure easier.

And guess what?

Over time, exposure builds up, and as more and more people interact with your content, the easier it becomes.
